Verbistro

Overview: Verbistro attempts to connect ESL teachers globally and allow them to buy and sell materials on an online marketplace. My goal was to redesign the web app to make it more user-friendly and appealing for users. The previous design wasn't the easiest to use nor was it very aesthetically pleasing. The I wanted to change that.

Research

In order to effectively design for the audience, I obviously had to get to know them. I did research on the market, competitors, and our audience to get a better understanding of my task.

Design

Starting off with basic sketches of the design was a necessary step in this process. From there, I was able to get a better idea of how I wanted the layout and general "feel" of the web app to be. From there, I began creating wireframes which did a better job of showing my ideas.

Analyze

Prototype

After additional research and analysis, I designed high fidelty prototypes using Photoshop and Affinity Designer. Afte

Test

These updates are currently being developed and is now in the testing phase to figure out what worked and what's left to improve on. Stay tuned for updates and feel free to check out the web app below!

Conclusion

Overall, this was a very interesting project and I got the opportunity to work with an audience I haven't yet - educators. After additional feedback, research, and brainstorming, I would still like to make additional changes to the web app. We will soon be undergoing our third iteration of the design so I'm looking forward to implementing changes and continuing to improve the design.
